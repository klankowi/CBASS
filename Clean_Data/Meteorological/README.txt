Folder contains processed and cleaned meterological data.

- Harpswell_Precipitation: Community Collaborative Rain, Hail & Snow network (CoCoRaHS) precipitation data from Harpswell Neck
- Portland_watertemp: NOAA Portland Harbor buoy surface water temperature
- Wiscasset_Airport_7dayavg_meterological: Rolling 7-day average meteorological conditions at Wiscasset Airport (for oyster index)
